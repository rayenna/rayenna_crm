# First Login Guide

## Welcome

Welcome to Rayenna Energy CRM! This guide will help you log in for the first time and get started with the system.

## Login Process

### Step 1: Access the Login Page

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
2. Navigate to the Rayenna CRM login page (URL provided by your administrator)
3. You'll see the login screen with email and password fields

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

1. **Email Address**: Enter the email address provided by your administrator
   - This is your username for the system
   - Use the exact email address as provided
   - The email is case-sensitive

2. **Password**: Enter the temporary password provided by your administrator
   - This is a temporary password you'll change after first login
   - Passwords are case-sensitive (check Caps Lock)
   - If you don't have a password, contact your administrator

3. **Click Login**: Click the **Login** button to access the system

### Step 3: First-Time Password Change

After your first successful login, you'll be prompted to change your password:

1. Enter your current (temporary) password
2. Enter your new password (must meet password requirements)
3. Confirm your new password by entering it again
4. Click **Save** or **Change Password**

**Important**: You must change your password before you can use the system.

## Password Rules

Your new password must meet these requirements:

- **Minimum length**: At least 8 characters (longer is recommended)
- **Complexity**: Include a mix of:
  - Uppercase letters (A-Z)
  - Lowercase letters (a-z)
  - Numbers (0-9)
  - Special characters (!, @, #, $, %, etc.)
- **Security**: 
  - Cannot be the same as your email address
  - Should be unique (not used elsewhere)
  - Avoid common words or personal information

### Password Best Practices

- Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Make it memorable but not easily guessable
- Don't use personal information (birthday, name, etc.)
- Consider using a passphrase (multiple words with numbers/symbols)
- Don't share your password with anyone
- Change your password regularly (every 90 days recommended)

### Password Examples

**Good passwords:**
- `Solar@2024Energy!`
- `MyCRM#Pass123`
- `Green$Power2024`

**Weak passwords to avoid:**
- `password123`
- `12345678`
- `yourname2024`

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

### Cannot Log In

**Problem**: Login fails or shows error message

**Solutions**:
1. Verify your email address is correct (check for typos)
2. Check that Caps Lock is not enabled
3. Ensure you're using the correct password
4. Try clearing your browser cache
5. Try a different browser
6. Contact your administrator if issues persist

### Forgot Password

**Problem**: You don't remember your password

**Solution**: 
- Contact your system administrator
- They can reset your password
- You'll receive a new temporary password
- Change it immediately after logging in

### Account Locked

**Problem**: Account is locked after multiple failed login attempts

**Solutions**:
1. Wait 10-15 minutes and try again
2. Contact your system administrator to unlock your account
3. Verify you're using the correct credentials
4. Check with administrator that your account is active

### Cannot Access Features

**Problem**: Menu items are missing or features are unavailable

**Solutions**:
1. Verify your user role has the necessary permissions
2. Check that you're assigned the correct role
3. Some features are role-specific
4. Contact your administrator if you need additional access

### Password Change Issues

**Problem**: Cannot change password or password doesn't meet requirements

**Solutions**:
1. Review password rules carefully
2. Ensure password meets all requirements
3. Check that new password and confirmation match
4. Try a different password that meets all criteria
5. Contact administrator if problems persist

## Whom to Contact for Access Issues

### System Administrator

For the following issues, contact your system administrator:

- **Account Creation**: Need a new user account
- **Password Reset**: Forgot password or account locked
- **Role Changes**: Need different permissions or role assignment
- **Access Issues**: Cannot access features you should have
- **Technical Problems**: System errors or technical issues
- **Account Status**: Questions about account activation or deactivation

### How to Contact

- **Email**: Use the contact information provided by your organization
- **Phone**: Call your IT support or system administrator
- **In-Person**: Visit your administrator's office if available

### Information to Provide

When contacting support, have ready:
- Your full name
- Your email address (username)
- Your user role
- Description of the issue
- Any error messages you've seen
- What you were trying to do when the issue occurred

## Security Reminders

- **Report suspicious activity** immediately
- **Keep your browser updated** for security
- **Don't save passwords** in browser on shared devices
