# Management User Guide

## Welcome

This guide is designed specifically for Management users of Rayenna Energy CRM. As a Management user, you have read-only access to comprehensive dashboards, reports, and analytics to help you make strategic business decisions.

## Your Role and Access

### Overview

Management users have executive-level access to view and analyze all business data without the ability to modify records. This read-only access ensures you can monitor performance, analyze trends, and make informed decisions while maintaining data integrity.

### Key Responsibilities

- **Strategic Monitoring**: Track company-wide performance metrics
- **Data Analysis**: Analyze trends and patterns across all departments
- **Decision Making**: Use insights to make informed business decisions
- **Performance Review**: Monitor team and departmental performance
- **Reporting**: Generate reports for stakeholders and board meetings

### Access Level

**What You Can Do**:
- View all dashboards and analytics
- Access all projects and customers
- View financial data and reports
- Analyze trends and patterns
- Export data for analysis
- Use filters to analyze specific periods

**What You Cannot Do**:
- Cannot create, edit, or delete any records
- Cannot update project information
- Cannot modify customer data
- Cannot update payments
- Cannot create support tickets
- Cannot manage users

## Your Dashboard

### Overview

Your Management Dashboard provides a comprehensive, aggregated view of all business metrics across Sales, Operations, and Finance departments.

### Key Metrics

**Top Row Metrics** (Company-Wide Overview):
- **Total Leads**: Total number of potential customers across all sales team
- **Total Capacity**: Combined system capacity (in kilowatts) across all projects
- **Total Revenue**: Total value of all projects in the system
- **Total Pipeline**: Combined value of all active projects (from Lead to Confirmed stages)
- **Total Profit**: Total profit across all completed projects

**Second Row Metrics** (Operational Status):
- **Pending Installation**: Number of projects waiting for installation
- **Pending Subsidy**: Number of projects waiting for subsidy credit
- **Subsidy Credited**: Number of projects where subsidy has been received

### Charts and Visualizations

**Project Value and Profit by Financial Year**:
- Visual representation of revenue and profitability trends over time
- Compare performance across different financial years
- Identify growth patterns and trends
- Make strategic planning decisions

**Project Value by Segment**:
- Breakdown of projects by type (Residential Subsidy, Commercial, etc.)
- Understand which segments drive revenue
- Identify market opportunities
- Plan resource allocation

**Customer Profitability Word Cloud**:
- Visual representation of most profitable customers
- Identify key customer relationships
- Focus on high-value customers
- Strategic customer management

**Sales Team Performance Treemap**:
- Visual representation of sales team performance
- Compare individual salesperson contributions
- Identify top performers
- Plan sales team development

### Using Dashboard Filters

**Financial Year Filter**:
- Select one or more financial years to analyze

**Month Filter**:

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot View Data

**Issue**: Cannot see projects or customers

**Solutions**:
- Verify you're logged in as Management user
- Check your role permissions
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact administrator

### Dashboard Not Loading

**Issue**: Dashboard metrics not displaying

**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Contact administrator

### Filters Not Working

**Issue**: Filters not applying correctly

**Solutions**:
- Clear all filters and reapply
- Check filter selections
- Refresh the page
- Try different filter combination
- Contact administrator

### Data Appears Incorrect

**Issue**: Metrics or data seem wrong

**Solutions**:
- Verify filters are correct
- Check date ranges
- Review data source
- Compare with other views
- Contact administrator to verify
